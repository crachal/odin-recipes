World's Best Lasagna

This lasagna is so satisfying and filling! It takes a little bit of time but is sooo worth it!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Sweet Italian sausage
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef and sausage until browned.
  2. Stir in tomato sauce
  3. Boil lasagna noodles for 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  5. Assemble meat sauce on the bottom of baking pan. Add noodles length wise. Spread ricotta cheese. Repeat layers and top off with mozzarella.
  6. Bake in preheated over for 25 minutes covered in foil. Then remove foil and back for additional 25 minutes. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes.
